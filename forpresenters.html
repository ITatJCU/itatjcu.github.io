NQ IT Industry Conference

Hosted by IT@JCU - June 26, 2015

Getting IT industry professionals together along with students for networking and great presentations and discussions.

This year's theme: Smarter North Queensland

Respond or enquire by email

Information For Presenters

We are seeking:

  • presentations focused on what's happening now using technology to make a smarter North Queensland, what the current needs and issues are and what the future will look like.
  • interesting exhibits/displays for the foyer. People will mingle and network around the displays during the breaks.

Details for Presentations

Where possible (unless there's a need otherwise), please have your presentation visuals online.
This means we can link to it and use the same computer with no changing inputs or loading files.
Consider using something like:

  • slides.com
  • Google Slides
  • Prezi

Your choice of hand-held or lapel wireless mic is available.

We will be recording the sessions. Please advise if you do not wish to have your presentation recorded or you do not wish to have it made public.

Details for Exhibitions

Wi-Fi, power and tables will be available. Just let us know what you need.
