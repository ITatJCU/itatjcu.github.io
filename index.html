NQ IT Industry Conference

Free event hosted by IT@JCU - June 26, 2015

Getting IT industry professionals together along with students for networking and great presentations and discussions.

This year's theme: Smarter North Queensland

Register now for the conference

Event Details

  • Date: Friday 26th of June, 2015
  • Time: 3pm - 7pm
  • Cost: $0 (free!)
  • Location: The event will be video-conferenced between Townsville and Cairns, with presenters in both cities:
    • Townsville: JCU Douglas Campus, 134-010 (Education Central building 134, room 010)
    • Cairns: JCU Smithfield Campus, A21.001 (building 21, room 001) TBC
  • Light refreshments provided.

This is an open and free event. Please invite anyone you think would be interested!

Tweet #itatjcu Use the Twitter hashtag, #itatjcu, to tweet comments or questions during/before/after the event.

Afterwards, please complete our short survey to provide feedback on the event and give comments to the presenters and organisers, thanks!
